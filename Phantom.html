How It Works

  1. Enter any website URL (http, https, www, etc.)
  2. Optionally customize the page title and favicon
  3. Choose a favicon source (URL or upload)
  4. Click "Load Favicon" to preview the favicon
  5. Click "Embed Website" to open it embedded in a new tab
  6. The website will appear to be hosted on about:blank
  7. Since the website is in about:blank, it is invisible
  8. About:blank doesn't show on your teachers computer

    9. Some websites won't embed due to their rules
